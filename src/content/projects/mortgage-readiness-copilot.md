The idea is to take advantage of Open Banking API: load the client's data from their accounts across banks and use agentic design to keep it user friendly, avoid manual, tedious and time-consuming process leading to lower willingness to buy mortgage.

B2B2C application to assess mortgage readiness — friendly for clients, appreciated by sellers.

Currently in exploring phase.
