The idea is to take advantage from Open banking API: load the client's data from their accounts across banks and use agentic design to keep it user friendly, avoid manual, tedious and time-consuming use of the app leading to lower willigness to buy mortgage.

It's a B2B2C application to assess mortgage readiness user friendly - for clients, appreciated by sellers.

It's currently in exploring phase.
